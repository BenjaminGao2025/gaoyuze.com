This book begins as a container for practice.

One thousand hours is not a magic number. It is a shape large enough to expose excuses and small enough to be counted. The project asks what happens when effort becomes visible, when hours are not merely spent but recorded, examined, and slowly improved.

The chapters will grow from essays, logs, and reflections. Some will stay rough for a while. That is part of the method.
